This is the help page

Autherization commands:
spotify login --> proceed login and save credentials
spotfiy logout

Play/Pause:
spotify pause
spotify resume
spotify play <song/artist>
spotify play playlist <playlist>
spotify get-current --> get the name of the current song
spotify shuffle --> play in shuffle
spotify play_radio <search_item>
spotify next
spotify previous
spotify play genre <genre> --> search the genre and play something random from the genre

Managing volume:
spotify volume up <value> --> default value = 10
spotify volume down
spotify volume set <value> --> value must be given
